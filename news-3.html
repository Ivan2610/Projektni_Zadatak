Could we see the Kings Canyon map in the Season 4 again?

Apex Legends players are calling for the return of the classic ‘Kings Canyon’ map after developers Respawn Entertainment removed it from the game for the start of Season 3: Meltdown.

Many Apex Legends players have been enjoying the change to the new World’s Edge map that was released in Season 3.

However, there are also many fans who would like to see the return of the arena they grew to love since the game first launched on February 4, 2019.

During the early stages of Season 3, players were able to return to Kings Canyon for a limited time in the Shadowfall LTM, but now they want the iconic map to return for good.

As Season 3 is starting to come to a close, one Reddit user u/jdsexy called for the return of the arena in Season 4 and received overwhelming support from others in the community.
